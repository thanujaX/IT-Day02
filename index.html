SIGIRIYA

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is a magnificent 5th-century rock fortress and palace in Sri Lanka’s Central Province. Designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it features advanced ancient hydraulic water gardens, vibrant historical frescoes, a polished Mirror Wall, and a summit palace reachable via approximately 1,200 steps
